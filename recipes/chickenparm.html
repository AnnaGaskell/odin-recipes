Chicken Parmigiana

Description

Chicken parmigiana, or chicken parmesan (Italian: pollo alla parmigiana), is a dish that consists of breaded chicken breast covered in tomato sauce and mozzarella, parmesan, or provolone cheese.

The dish originated from 20th-century Italian diaspora. It has been speculated that the dish is based on a combination of the Italian melanzane alla Parmigiana, a dish using breaded eggplant slices, with a cotoletta, a breaded veal cutlet generally served without sauce or cheese in Italy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 3 tablespoons soffrito
  • 1 cup seasoned italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmigiano reggiano
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups of pasta sauce
  • handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cups sliced mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Coat sliced chicken breast in flour, then beaten eggs, then breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry in olive oil 2 minutes on each side until goden, set aside.
  3. Coat bottom of 9 by 13 inch pan in 1 cup of sauce.
  4. Lay fried chicken in sauce without layering.
  5. Lightly top with 1/2 cup sauce.
  6. Lay slices of mozzarella on top of chicken and sauce, sprinkle with parmigiano.i>
  7. Cook at 350 for 20 minutes.
